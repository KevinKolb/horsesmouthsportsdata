Easy Sports Data for Everyone

No coding knowledge? No problem! We provide real sports data from official sources like NFL, NBA, MLB, and more. Our data comes in simple formats that anyone can use - whether you're building an app, doing research, or just curious about sports.

Perfect for learning! This data is ideal for learning databases and programming, and we refresh it frequently to keep it current. Some information comes directly from official league sources, while other data points are carefully researched and manually entered by our team.

What is XML? Think of it like a digital filing cabinet. XML organizes information in a way that's easy for both humans and computers to read. It's similar to HTML (what makes websites), but designed specifically for storing and sharing data.
